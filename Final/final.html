Visit Seattle!

Welcome to my Web 1.0 Website featuring Seattle attractions to visit!

This maps intended audience is for tourists visiting Seattle.
It starts at the airport, and highlights the route to the city center.
There, tourists spots are highlighted with points, and some POIs are highlighted with polygons.
I chose this basemap because the real one I wanted, Thunderforest, requires an API key, and I wasn't going to do that just for a small project.
This basemap doesn't require a key, shows labels, has nice colors, and has general modes of travel
without being overly distracting. This was a good neutral, showing the relavent content like roads and parks, while having subdued colors.
As you can see, I've used several differnt plugins here, some more visible then others.
I had a very hard time with some of them due to poor documentation and abandoned projects that don't work anymore.
However, I've gotten everything at least into a working state that meets the critera. If given extra time (and money) I'd design an animated story map.
The last problem I had was with popup content. It's very difficult and time consuming to escape all the characters to embed HTML,
and get the popups resized to fit content properly. Nothing here is automatic. I would like everypop up to have special content,
but it's just too time consuming and tedious.
